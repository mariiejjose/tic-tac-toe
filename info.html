Welcome!!!!!!!!!

This is a Christmas-Themed Tic Tac Toe game, or as most of us Lebanese call it, a game of XO. While most people view this game as a way to pass time in class with their friends (same goes for me), it is actually a fun game and a big part of out childhood. So, seeing as we had a Web Development project, what better thing to present other than a fun and easy game! We hope our fellow classmates and Dr. Hicham like it.
Thank you for playing :)
